About

VOCAL • A voice for the unheard -a student-led initiative for the underprivileged We provide students the exposure they need to become proficient in their choice of field
-VOCAL mainly focuses on imparting skill And overall development of the student. the field of their choice.
VOCAL provides students education, That goes beyond the boundaries of academics we proffer them career guidance, sports, music, art, soft skills, and many other sessions!


Objective and Goals

OBJECTIVE - To develop the underprivileged Inclusive of their interests and help them to transform their hidden skills into a liveable future.
GOALS
In 3 months we plan to:
1.gathering 100 interns
2.cover a total of 5-6 schools and Orphanage
3.reach out to a large audience through social media handles.


More

WORKING OF VOCAL — We plan to gather interns diverse approaches to contribute towards a strong foundation organization. — our mission is to integrate people with different ideologies to reach the goal of our organisation —we plan to train our interns by making them attend a few sessions and giving them a clear and concise guide to how they are going to work for vocal — online and offline sessions with our interns will be held to discuss the further actions to be taken to help these students Our team is planning to work towards spreading awareness among the underprivileged to empower themselves and help them work towards a better tomorrow.
